# Privacy Policy — Mist Carver

*Last updated: 17 April 2026*

Mist Carver ("the app") is developed by Scabo (the "developer"). This privacy policy describes how the app handles data.

## Summary

Mist Carver does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Details

- The app runs entirely offline. No network requests are made to developer-controlled servers.
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are integrated.
- No user accounts are required.
- All game progress (save files) is stored locally on the user's device, inside the app's sandbox, and never transmitted.
- The app uses standard Apple system services (VoiceOver, CoreHaptics, AVAudioEngine) which are governed by Apple's own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to this Policy

Any future changes to this policy will be reflected on this page, with an updated revision date.
